How it works

Three steps.
One card. Full safety.

01

Add your allergens

Tap to select what you're allergic to. Set severity — anaphylactic, severe, or intolerance. Add specific triggers if needed.

02

Generate your card

Built instantly in your chosen language and English. Hidden ingredients are included automatically — things like shrimp paste in Thai food, or dashi in Japanese broth.

03

Show it at the table

One tap opens the card full-screen. Hand your phone to any server or street vendor. No translation app. No guesswork. No risk of misunderstanding.

Who's behind this

Built by someone
who actually eats here.

I'm Abe — a healthcare professional living and working in Bangkok. I built AllergyPass because I watched people struggle to communicate food allergies in kitchens where nobody spoke English and translation apps got the nuance wrong.

The translations are verified by hand, not generated. The hidden ingredients are there because I know the cuisine — not because an algorithm guessed.

On Instagram I share what it's actually like to live here — the observations, the systems, the things that don't make it into any guidebook.
