How it works

Built for the moments when

🔍

Hidden Trigger Detection

The card flags allergens that don't appear on menus: shrimp paste in curries, peanut oil in satay, fish sauce in nearly everything. What you need to know, in the language that matters.

🌓

Chef-Ready Mode

Switch to full-screen, high-contrast mode with large Thai script and bold borders. Instantly readable at arm's length in a busy kitchen or dim restaurant.

✈️

Always Offline

Once built, your card works in airplane mode. No roaming fees, no signal required. Ready when you need it most.
