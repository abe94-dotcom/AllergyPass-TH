Who is Abe

A dentist who moved to Bangkok
and started writing things down

I'm a dentist living in Bangkok. I write about food, health, and daily life here because most of what I found online when I moved was either wrong, outdated, or written by someone who spent two weeks here.

The allergy card came first — I built it for friends who couldn't safely eat out. The blog is everything else I wish someone had told me.
